About CyberCell VIIT

CyberCell VIIT is a tight-knit group of students aiming to study and learn more about the CyberSecurity domain, and with this aim, we are constantly trying to upgrade ourselves by working collaboratively on various topics.

VIIT has established a CYBER-CELL at the institute level in December 2013. Society at large and specifically the IT fraternity should be aware of security breaches on computer networks and the consequences of cyber-attacks in general.

We should have the relevant knowledge to detect and curb menaces like cyber pornography, email spoofing, spamming, bombing, online shopping frauds, credit card frauds, computer security issues, romance/dating scam, banking/credit card related crimes, identity thefts, safety in banking and social networking site, safety tips for mobile phone users, cyberstalking, call SMS forging, etc.


Types of Questions

These are some of the types of questions that may be in this CTF

Cryptography

Cryptography is a method of protecting information and communications through the use of codes, so that only those for whom the information is intended can read and process it.

Web Challenges

These are the vulnerabilities the often show up in CTFs as web security challenges where the user needs to exploit a bug to gain some kind of higher level privelege and get the flag.

Steganography

Steganography is the technique of hiding secret data within an ordinary, non-secret, file or message in order to avoid detection; the secret data is then extracted at its destination

Reverse Engg.

Reverse engineering, sometimes called back engineering, is a process in which software and other products are deconstructed to extract design information from them in order to get a flag.
